Making Videos with FFmpeg
Recently, I helped someone to make youtube videos. Originally I used an interface software but the free version has a lot of limitations. Plus, using a command-line software (like ffmpeg) can be faster for certain functions. Here is a very detailed tutorial on making simple videos using ffmpeg (like overlaying music, video and images). By the way, I leanrt something very important in this project - reencoding, but I cannot share the secret here!

git clone https://github.com/poonh/ffmpeg_video.git

Then open video_script.ipybn with Jupyter Notebook and you are there. You can also use Github Codespace to run it.

Oral History - An Ordinary Family Under Khmer Rouge (东广岛华人红色高棉证言)
This is my first and only project in oral history. I know a family in Higashihiroshima(東広島), Japan who are refugess of the Khmer Rouge (the bloody regime in Cambodia from 1975-1979). I documented their lives before, during and after Khmer Rouge. This story is unlike any other stories you find online. This one is far less bloddy. What I want to know is their lives under the regime, and how it changed the family history, but not to learn about the "bloodiness" of the regime.
The story is originally in Chinese, and machine translated into Japanese.
